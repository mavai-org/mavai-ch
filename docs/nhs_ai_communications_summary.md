## Overall interpretation

Taken together, these communications show the NHS discussing AI in four broad modes:

1. **Policy and governance** — rules, information governance, and responsible adoption.
2. **Procurement and implementation** — practical guidance for selecting and deploying AI tools.
3. **Operational deployment** — announcements about concrete NHS AI use cases.
4. **Ethics, assurance, and trust** — evaluation, confidence, public legitimacy, and data stewardship.
